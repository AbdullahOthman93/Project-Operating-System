# docs/examples — Worked examples

**Purpose:** Holds illustrative, worked examples for onboarding — sample workflows, sample records, sample contract usage.

**Scope:** Illustrative only. Examples have no independent authority and must never be treated as a substitute for `docs/architecture/`, `docs/kernel/`, `docs/standards/`, or `docs/contracts/`.

**Contents:**
- `OnboardingGuide.md` — non-authoritative "start here" path for a new participant (human or AI), pointing at the documents that govern the work; leads into the authoritative entry point `docs/architecture/ArchitectureOverview.md` and restates nothing.
- `SprintWalkthrough.md` — non-authoritative worked example following one real, already-closed sprint (Sprint 2.1) end to end; a historical illustration drawn from the permanent record, not live status.

**Dependencies:** None as an authority. As a Supporting directory, its examples *reference* material across all other `docs/` areas — reference only, per the Dependency Model (rule 5), and conformance references per rule 7: `docs/architecture/`, `docs/kernel/`, `docs/contracts/`, `docs/standards/`, `docs/runtime/`, `docs/knowledge/` (the real record the walkthrough cites), and the supporting `docs/operations/`, `docs/agents/`, and `docs/templates/`. No document depends on this directory.

**Related documents:** every other `INDEX.md` under `docs/`.
